Why did I create glide?

I needed simple and fast slider with fully customizable OOCSS markup. There was actually any that would meet my needs.
So it came about, Glide. It’s designed to slide. No less, no more.

Responsive & touch-friendly

Yup! It works on smartphones,
tablets and desktops.

CSS3

CSS3 Transitions, but don’t worry, there is fallback to older browsers.
